Sally Paganin

Assistant Professor @ The Ohio State University

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at The Ohio State University. Prior to that, I was a Research Fellow in the Department of Biostatistics at Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health, working with Jeff Miller on statistical methods for early cancer detection. I also did a postdoc at UC Berkeley, where I worked with Perry de Valpine & Chris Paciorek on computational aspects of Bayesian models.
I am an active collaborator to NIMBLE, a flexible R-based software for hierarchical models (and more!).

Here you can find some info about me, my research, and my CV.

You can email me at paganin.1@osu.edu.
